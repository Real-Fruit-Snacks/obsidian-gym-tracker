A training log that belongs to you

From the first set to the long view.

Build a workout from scratch or load a routine, mark sets as you complete them, run a rest timer, and come back later to see the history in context. Gym Tracker keeps the workflow focused without turning your vault into a spreadsheet.

Ruck tracking

Let your GPS app record. Let Obsidian remember.

Import a GPX file and Gym Tracker fills the route details it can calculate. Add pack weight and perceived effort, then save a complete training record alongside the rest of your notes.

Built for the screen in your hand

One plugin, both places.

Log in the gym on mobile. Review, organize, and export from desktop.

Local by design

Your training data is not a product.

Gym Tracker stores workout data through Obsidian and reads GPX files locally. It does not require an account or send workout and location data to an external service.

Export a JSON backup or create Markdown workout notes whenever you want a portable copy.

Install

Bring Gym Tracker into your vault.

Download the three release files, place them in the plugin folder, and enable Gym Tracker from Obsidian settings.
